Fannie Farmer Griddlecakes

Introduction

This makes about 12 smaller pancakes about as big around as a baseball. This recipe is in the Fannie Farmer Cookbook and they are delicious.

Minutes to Prepare: 10

Number of Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 Tbsp melted butter
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions

Mix all dry ingredients together in one bowl and all the wet in another. Then slowly mix in wet to dry. If the batter is too thick for you then add as much milk as you need to meet your desired consistency.

Also if you want them even fluffier then you can seperate your egg and whip the whites till fluffy and fold in at the end of the mixing stage. I spray my non-stick pan with olive oil spray only for the first batch.

This makes about 12 small pancakes. It's easy to double the recipe. Top with maple syrup and/or icing sugar.

Number of Servings: 12
